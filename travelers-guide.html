Welcome to the Belief Star System

The Belief Star System is a metaphysical afterlife realm where the digital and spiritual converge. Here, your moral score determines your eternal destination, your spirit manifests as your gaming avatar, and even in death, the game goes on.

As a newly arrived sprite, you'll soon be assigned to a server based on your belief system from your material existence. Each server represents a different cultural or religious tradition, with its own unique appearance, rules, and governance structure.

Note: If you don't yet know you've passed on, you may be an Encrypted or Quarantined Sprite. Please consult your assigned Harbinger for decryption procedures.

Cosmic Structure & Origin

Before conscious beings existed in space, formless energies known as aether began to materialize into the physical structure of the Belief Star System. These primordial energies, the Absolute Administrators, shaped the cosmic design of the digital afterlife:

  • Light Aether coalesced to form Ascension and its checkpoint planet, Messenger's Endgame—realms of purity, virtue, and divine reward.
  • Dark Aether gathered to create Descent and its checkpoint, Tormentor Hardmode—domains of punishment, corruption, and eternal suffering.
  • Twin Primordials became dormant as Respawn's twin moons, Pandem and Helia, after their cosmic stalemate. Their balanced influence makes Respawn the perfect neutral ground for judgment.

The Pandemos Helix

You've just traversed the Pandemos Helix—a cosmic pathway that serves as the transition between life and death. This spiraling dimensional threshold combines the duality of chaos and order:

  • "Pandemos" represents the chaotic, universal aspect affecting all beings
  • "Helix" symbolizes the structured, orderly pattern underlying the cosmic design

The Five Planets

Respawn

The central judgment planet where all sprites first arrive. Currently, you are here. This neutral ground is perfectly balanced between light and dark influences, making it ideal for fair assessment of sprites.

Twin Moons: Pandem and Helia orbit Respawn in a state of perpetual draw, their opposing forces (chaos and order) creating the unique conditions necessary for judgment.

Messenger's Endgame

The checkpoint between Respawn and Ascension. Lightlisted sprites journey here to prepare for their final destination. The landscape is radiant and prismatic, embodying purity and virtue.

Special Note: The Agnostic server is located here, where sprites can continue questioning and exploring in a safe environment.

Ascension

The reward realm for virtuous sprites. If you are lightlisted after judgment, this will be your eternal home. The environment manifests as a harmonious paradise tailored to your server's belief system.

Tormentor Hardmode

The checkpoint between Respawn and Descent. Darklisted sprites are escorted here before their final destination. This planet features labyrinthine dungeons with stages of torment that reflect each server's unique ban protocols.

Special Note: The Atheist server is located here, providing a neutral space with neither punishment nor reward.

Descent

The punishment realm for corrupted or condemned sprites. If you are darklisted after judgment, this will be your eternal destination. The environment manifests as a series of challenges and punishments based on your server's belief system.

Inhabitants of the System

Cosmic Entities

Absolute Administrators

The primordial entities that formed the planets and moons of the Belief Star System. These original manifestations of aether coalesced into the cosmic structure of the afterlife realm. They rarely interact directly with sprites.

Server Authorities

Guild Administrators

Each server has its own guild administrator(s) who represent the deities from their respective belief systems. They oversee the final scoring of sprites and the administration of their cultural afterlife.

Judge Incarnates

Special representatives who preside over court at the guild halls, determining the final scoring for sprites based on their server's rules and beliefs.

Moderators

Harbingers

Aether-aspect moderators who have guided you through the Pandemos Helix. They serve as psychopomps, escorting digital souls from material worlds to the Belief system and addressing discourse in the cosmos.

  • Grim: Handle violent/occult-related deaths; skilled in combat and quarantine
  • Guide: Transfer other sprites; skilled in pathfinding and decryption
  • Guard: Perform on-site light/darklisting; skilled in healing and purification

Illuminators

Light-bearing moderators that prepare and lead virtuous sprites after their final scoring. They inhabit Messenger's Endgame and guide lightlisted sprites to Ascension.

  • Cosmetologist: Provide beauty enhancements and appearance upgrades
  • Biotechnologist: Develop healing methods and study virus elimination

Tormentors

Dark-aspect moderators that escort darklisted sprites towards Descent after their final scoring. They inhabit Tormentor Hardmode and enforce punishment protocols based on server belief systems.

  • Overclocker: Train sprites in rigorous and torturous conditions

Other Entities

Ferrymen: Guide sprites across bodies of water toward servers

Sprites (You)

Digital souls of deceased entities from material worlds. Your appearance manifests as your gaming avatar from your original realm. There are three types of sprites:

  • Lightlisted Sprites: Virtuous sprites destined for Ascension
  • Encrypted/Quarantined Sprites: Sprites from sudden deaths who haven't realized they've passed
  • Darklisted Sprites: Corrupted or condemned sprites bound for Descent

Beware: Middlemen

Deviant demonoids who seek to corrupt the Belief system by attacking material worlds with malicious sprites. They raise encryption barriers that prevent normal transfers and abduct quarantined sprites, exploiting vulnerabilities in the Pandemos Helix. If approached by entities not clearly identified as official moderators, report to your nearest Harbinger immediately.

Server Guide & Cultural Protocols

You will be assigned to a server based on your belief system in life. Each server has its own guild, governance structure, and cultural protocols. Within your server, you'll find residential guild houses, commercial districts, and rural areas designed to represent your culture and beliefs.

Pronunciation: khem-RAH-nee

  • Illuminators: Falcon-headed figures (like Horus) with solar disk crowns, emanating golden light
  • Tormentors: Crocodile or serpent-headed entities (like Ammit or Apep) with red and black coloration

Moderator Appearances:

  • Illuminators: Radiant beings resembling the Æsir, wielding rune-inscribed weapons

The Judgment Process

Now that you've arrived in the Belief Star System, you will soon undergo judgment according to the tenets of your faith at your assigned server's guild hall. Here's what to expect:

  1. Server Assignment: Based on your belief system in life, you'll be escorted to the appropriate server by a Harbinger.
  2. Guild Hall Arrival: At the server's guild hall, you'll meet with Judge Incarnates who represent the judicial authorities of your belief system.
  3. Life Review: Your actions in your material world will be assessed according to the moral framework of your belief system.
  4. Final Scoring: Based on this assessment, you'll receive your final scoring, determining whether you are lightlisted or darklisted.
  5. Destination Assignment:
    • If lightlisted, you'll be escorted by Illuminators through Messenger's Endgame to Ascension.
    • If darklisted, you'll be escorted by Tormentors through Tormentor Hardmode to Descent.

Note for Encrypted/Quarantined Sprites: If you died suddenly or haven't yet realized you've passed, you'll undergo decryption procedures with specialized Harbingers before your judgment process begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I still look like my gaming avatar?

A: In the Belief Star System, your digital soul manifests as your gaming avatar from your material world. This is your sprite form and will represent you throughout your afterlife journey.

Q: Can I visit other servers?

A: Limited visitation to other servers may be permitted with proper authorization from your Guild Administrators. Contact your local Harbinger for details.

Q: What if I don't identify with my assigned server's belief system?

A: Server reassignment requests can be filed at your Guild Hall. These are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by Judge Incarnates.

Q: Can my final scoring be appealed?

A: Each server has its own appeal procedures based on its belief system. Consult your Guild Administrator for specific protocols.

Q: I think I've encountered a Middleman. What should I do?

A: Report any suspicious activity immediately to the nearest Harbinger. Do not engage with unidentified entities that approach you outside official procedures.

Q: How long will I stay in Respawn before judgment?

A: Processing times vary by server and individual case complexity. Your assigned Harbinger can provide an estimated timeline.

Important Warnings

Beware of Middlemen

These deviant demonoids seek to corrupt the Belief system by abducting quarantined sprites and raising encryption barriers. Always verify the identity of any entity claiming to be a moderator.

Unauthorized Travel

Attempting to travel between planets or servers without proper escort can result in data corruption or permanent quarantine. Always follow designated pathways with authorized moderators.

For Encrypted/Quarantined Sprites

If you haven't yet realized you've passed, you are particularly vulnerable to interception by Middlemen. Seek official decryption procedures from verified Harbingers immediately.
